# Shoulder pain (common buckets)
Not a diagnosis. Common possibilities include:
- Rotator cuff / tendinopathy-type irritation
- Impingement-like symptoms (painful arc, overhead discomfort)
- Instability-type symptoms (feels like slipping, history of dislocation)

Conservative next steps:
- Reduce overhead/high-tension moves temporarily (big gastons, hard lock-offs)
- Keep pulling volume moderate and pain-limited
- Gradual strengthening of rotator cuff + scapular control

Consider evaluation if:
- instability episodes, significant weakness, or pain after trauma
