# General climbing load management
Educational only. Conservative guidance for overuse-type pain.

- Reduce intensity and volume first (especially max hangs, hard crimping, limit boulders)
- Keep pain during activity low (commonly <= 3/10) and avoid sharp pain
- Prefer frequent, easier sessions over rare, maximal sessions
- Progress gradually (increase one variable at a time: intensity OR volume OR frequency)
- Sleep, nutrition, and stress strongly affect recovery
- If pain persists >2–3 weeks despite load reduction, get evaluated
