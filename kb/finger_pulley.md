# Finger pain during crimping (common buckets)
Not a diagnosis. Common possibilities include:
- Flexor pulley strain/irritation (often pain on palmar side near A2/A4 region)
- Flexor tendon irritation (tender along tendon, worse with repeated gripping)
- Joint capsule irritation (pain around joint line, stiffness)

Conservative next steps:
- Avoid max crimp and dynamic catches for 1–2 weeks
- Use open-hand grip when possible
- Gradual return: easy climbing -> moderate -> harder, only if symptoms stay mild
- Consider taping as a comfort measure (not a guarantee)

Consider evaluation if:
- audible pop + immediate swelling/bruising
- obvious bowstringing, marked weakness, or worsening function
