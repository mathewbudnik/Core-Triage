# Elbow pain (common overuse buckets)
Not a diagnosis. Common possibilities include:
- Lateral elbow tendinopathy (“tennis elbow”) often worse with gripping + wrist extension
- Medial elbow tendinopathy (“golfer’s elbow”) often worse with pulling + wrist flexion/pronation
- Nerve irritation (tingling/numbness)

Conservative next steps:
- Reduce hard gripping and high-volume steep pulling temporarily
- Use pain-limited isometrics and gradual strengthening
- Address technique and workload spikes (new board phase, sudden volume increase)

Consider evaluation if:
- numbness/tingling, weakness, or persistent night pain
