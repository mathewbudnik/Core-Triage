# Red flags (seek medical care urgently)
Educational only. If any are present, consider urgent evaluation.

- Deformity, obvious dislocation, or inability to move the joint normally
- Sudden loss of strength or “pop” with immediate swelling/bruising
- Severe pain at rest, rapidly worsening swelling, or spreading redness/fever
- Numbness/tingling, loss of sensation, or weakness in hand/arm
- Finger stuck in bent position, locking/catching that is new and painful
- Night pain that is severe and persistent, unexplained weight loss, or systemic illness
- Symptoms after major trauma/fall
